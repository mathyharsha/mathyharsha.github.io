## Tips for Creating Publication Images:

1. **Use figures from your papers**: Extract a key figure or diagram that represents the work
2. **Create simple diagrams**: Use tools like PowerPoint, Keynote, or draw.io
3. **Keep it simple**: The image is small (160x160px), so avoid complex details
4. **Use consistent style**: Similar colors, fonts, and layout across all images
5. **Placeholder option**: Use a solid color square with the paper acronym (e.g., "WIMF", "WIRL") in large text
